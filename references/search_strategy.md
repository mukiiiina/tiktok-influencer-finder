## Tips for Effective Searching

1. Start broad, then narrow: Begin with general niche keywords, then refine based on initial results
2. Use multiple languages: Even for English-market campaigns, Chinese keywords may surface additional creators
3. Check recency: Prioritize ranking lists from the current or previous year
4. Cross-reference: Creators appearing on multiple lists are likely more established
5. Look at engagement, not just followers: A 50K creator with 5% engagement often outperforms a 500K creator with 0.5% engagement
6. Check content consistency: Ensure the creator regularly posts content in the target niche
